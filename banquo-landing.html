What Banquo believes

Theatre software,
finally.

Every theatre company we've ever met runs on six spreadsheets, a group chat, and someone's memory. Banquo is the ghost at the table, quietly keeping it all straight, so the art is the thing everyone gets to focus on.

i.

One Scene Builder feeds every department.

Enter a scene once. Lighting, Sound, Props, Wardrobe, Set, and your Stage Manager all see it, structured the way they actually work, not the way the database feels like it.

ii.

Production, fundraising, and volunteers in one house.

Donors, ticket sales, grants, volunteer shifts, and board dashboards live beside rehearsal reports and cue sheets, not in a separate tool you forget to update.

iii.

GhostLight AI, on stage with you.

Our embedded AI assistant drafts acknowledgment letters, flags budget variance, recommends cue placements, and answers "when does Act II, Scene 3 break?" in plain language.
